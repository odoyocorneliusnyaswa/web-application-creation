About Our Lady of Perpetual Help

Nurturing young women to become leaders of tomorrow since 1970

Our History

Our Lady of Perpetual Help, Ahero Girls' High School was founded in 1970 by the Sisters of Mercy, a Catholic religious congregation dedicated to serving those in need through education. The school began with just 40 students and 4 teachers in a small building donated by the local parish.

Under the leadership of Sister Mary Catherine, the first principal, the school quickly established a reputation for academic excellence and character formation. By 1980, the school had grown to over 200 students and had constructed its first permanent dormitory and classroom block.

Throughout the 1980s and 1990s, the school continued to expand its facilities and enhance its academic programs. In 1995, the school achieved national school status in recognition of its outstanding academic performance and contribution to education in Kenya.

Today, Our Lady of Perpetual Help stands as one of the premier girls' schools in the region, with modern facilities, a comprehensive curriculum, and a continued commitment to nurturing young women who will make a positive impact on society.

While the school has grown significantly since its humble beginnings, it remains true to its founding principles of providing quality education rooted in Catholic values, promoting academic excellence, and fostering the holistic development of each student.

Our Mission

To provide quality education that nurtures academically competent, morally upright, and socially responsible young women who will contribute positively to society and become agents of change in their communities.

Our Vision

To be a center of excellence in girls' education, recognized nationally and internationally for producing holistically developed women leaders who are guided by faith, integrity, and service to humanity.

Our Core Values

Faith

We nurture spiritual growth and a deep commitment to Catholic values and principles that guide ethical decision-making and moral living.

Excellence

We pursue the highest standards in academics and all endeavors, encouraging students to develop their full potential and strive for continuous improvement.

Integrity

We promote honesty, accountability, and ethical conduct in all interactions, fostering a community built on trust and respect.

Service

We instill a commitment to serving others and making a positive difference in the community through compassionate action and leadership.

Community

We foster a sense of belonging and mutual support, celebrating diversity and promoting collaboration among students, staff, parents, and alumni.

Innovation

We encourage creative thinking, problem-solving, and adaptability to prepare students for the challenges of a rapidly changing world.

Principal's Message

Dear Parents, Students, and Visitors,

It is with great pleasure that I welcome you to Our Lady of Perpetual Help, Ahero Girls' High School. For over 50 years, our institution has been committed to providing quality education to young women in Kenya.

At Ahero Girls, we believe in nurturing the whole person - intellectually, spiritually, physically, and emotionally. Our dedicated team of educators works tirelessly to create a supportive learning environment where each student can discover her unique talents and develop the skills needed to succeed in an ever-changing world.

We are proud of our rich heritage and the achievements of our students and alumni who have gone on to make significant contributions in various fields. Our academic excellence is complemented by a strong emphasis on character formation, leadership development, and service to others.

As we look to the future, we remain committed to our founding principles while embracing innovation and best practices in education. We continue to invest in our facilities, enhance our curriculum, and provide diverse opportunities for our students to grow and excel.

I invite you to explore our website and learn more about our academic programs, extracurricular activities, and the vibrant community that makes Ahero Girls a special place to learn and grow. Should you have any questions or wish to visit our campus, please do not hesitate to contact us.

Thank you for your interest in Our Lady of Perpetual Help, Ahero Girls' High School. We look forward to partnering with you in the education and development of the next generation of women leaders.

Our Achievements

Our Lady of Perpetual Help, Ahero Girls' High School has a long-standing tradition of excellence in academics, sports, and co-curricular activities. Our students consistently perform well in national examinations and competitions, reflecting our commitment to quality education and holistic development.

Academic Excellence

  • Consistently ranked among the top 50 schools nationally in KCSE results
  • Over 90% university admission rate for the past 10 years
  • National champions in Science Congress competitions (2018, 2021)
  • Regional Mathematics Olympiad winners (2019, 2022)

Sports

  • National netball champions (2017, 2020)
  • Regional athletics champions (2019, 2021, 2022)
  • County basketball champions for five consecutive years (2018-2022)
  • National cross-country finalists (2021)

Co-curricular Activities

  • National Music Festival winners in choral verse (2019, 2022)
  • National Drama Festival finalists (2018, 2020)
  • Regional Debate Competition champions (2021)
  • National Environmental Conservation Award (2020)
