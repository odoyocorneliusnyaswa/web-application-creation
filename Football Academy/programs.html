Training Programs

At Elite Football Academy, we offer a range of programs designed to develop players at every stage of their football journey. Our structured curriculum ensures progressive skill development, tactical understanding, and physical conditioning appropriate for each age group.

Ages 6-12

Youth Development Program

Building fundamental skills and fostering a love for the game in a fun, engaging environment. Our youth program focuses on developing technical abilities, basic tactical concepts, and physical literacy through age-appropriate activities and small-sided games.

Schedule

2-3 sessions per week, 60-90 minutes each

Group Size

Maximum 12 players per coach

Focus Areas

Technical foundations, motor skills, game understanding, teamwork

Curriculum Highlights

  • Ball mastery and control
  • Passing and receiving techniques
  • Dribbling and 1v1 skills
  • Basic shooting mechanics
  • Small-sided games (3v3, 4v4, 5v5)
  • Introduction to positions and roles

Frequently Asked Questions

How do I know which program is right for my child?

We recommend starting with the program that matches your child's age group. However, we also offer free assessment sessions where our coaches can evaluate your child's current skill level and recommend the most appropriate program. Some players may be placed in programs outside their age group based on their development level.

What equipment does my child need?

Players should come to training with appropriate football boots/cleats, shin guards, water bottle, and weather-appropriate training attire. For matches, players will need the academy uniform kit which can be purchased through our online store. Goalkeepers should also have appropriate gloves.

Are there tryouts for the programs?

Our Youth Development Program is open to all players without tryouts. The Teen Elite and Performance Academy programs do require an assessment/tryout to ensure players are placed in an environment that matches their current ability level. We hold regular tryout sessions throughout the year, and players can also arrange for individual assessments.

What is your make-up session policy?

If a player misses a session due to illness or other commitments, they can attend a make-up session within the same month, subject to availability. Please contact our administrative team to schedule a make-up session. For weather cancellations, we automatically reschedule sessions and notify all participants.

Do you offer financial assistance?

Yes, we have a scholarship program for families who demonstrate financial need. Our goal is to make quality football training accessible to talented and committed players regardless of financial circumstances. Please contact our administrative office for more information about the application process.

How do you handle player evaluations and feedback?

All players receive regular feedback from their coaches during training sessions. Additionally, we conduct formal evaluations quarterly for all programs, which include written assessments and development goals. For Teen Elite and Performance Academy players, these evaluations also include video analysis and performance metrics. Parents can schedule meetings with coaches to discuss their child's progress.
