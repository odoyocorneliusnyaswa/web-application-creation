Frequently Asked Questions

How much does it cost to list my car?

Basic listings are free for 30 days. We also offer premium packages starting at $30 that include enhanced visibility, longer listing duration, and additional features to help sell your car faster.

How long will it take to sell my car?

Selling time varies based on factors like vehicle condition, price, demand, and location. Well-priced vehicles with good photos and descriptions typically sell within 2-3 weeks. Premium listings often sell faster due to increased visibility.

Is my personal information secure?

Yes, we take privacy seriously. Your contact information is only shared with serious buyers through our secure messaging system. You control how and when potential buyers can contact you.

Can I edit my listing after it's published?

Yes, you can edit your listing details, add or remove photos, and adjust the price at any time through your account dashboard. Keeping your listing updated with accurate information can help attract more buyers.

How do I handle the transaction safely?

We recommend meeting in a public place for test drives, verifying the buyer's identity, accepting secure payment methods (cashier's check, bank transfer, or cash), and properly transferring the title and registration. Our support team can provide guidance throughout the process.
