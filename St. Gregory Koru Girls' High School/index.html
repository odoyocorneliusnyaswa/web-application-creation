Welcome Message from the Principal

Dear Parents, Students, and Visitors,

It is with great pleasure that I welcome you to St. Gregory Koru Girls' High School. For over 40 years, our institution has been committed to providing quality education to young women in Kenya.

At St. Gregory Koru, we believe in nurturing the whole person - intellectually, spiritually, physically, and emotionally. Our dedicated team of educators works tirelessly to create a supportive learning environment where each student can discover her unique talents and develop the skills needed to succeed in an ever-changing world.

We invite you to explore our website and learn more about our academic programs, extracurricular activities, and the vibrant community that makes St. Gregory Koru a special place to learn and grow.

Mrs. Mercy Ollando

Principal
